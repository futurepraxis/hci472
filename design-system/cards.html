Card Components

Bootstrap classes for width, margin, and padding are noted for each individual component as needed.

Card with Image

Image card is used on the homepage to display links to featured portfolio pieces.

  • Width: col-md-4 col-sm-6
  • Margin-top: mt-5
  • Margin-bottom: mb-5
  • Padding: p-0

Card title

If Lacanist obscurity holds, the works of Fellini are postmodern.

Go somewhere

Code Snippet

                          
<div class="card DS-card col-md-4 col-sm-6 p-0 mt-5 mb-5">
<img src="../images/design-system/slide1.png" class="card-img-top" alt="...">
<div class="card-body">
<h3 class="card-title DS-h3">Card title</h3>
<p class="card-text">If Lacanist obscurity holds, the works of Fellini are postmodern.</p>
<a href="#" class="btn btn-primary DS-button">Go somewhere</a>
</div>
</div>

Card without Image

The imageless card can be used on portfolio pages to call out important information such as Team Members, Project Goals, and Outcomes.

  • Width: col-md-4 col-sm-6
  • Margin-top: mt-5
  • Margin-bottom: mb-5
  • Padding: p-0
