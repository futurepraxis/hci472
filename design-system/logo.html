Logo

Find information on how to use the logo below. (NB: These logos are actually pretty awful and probably won't be used outside of this project.

Logo Type Usage Example
Primary

This is the primary logo that should be used. It should appear prominently on any official communications, such as the website homepage. The logo should not be altered.

Primary with Dropshadow

May be used if more emphasis on the logo is desired.
Secondary

The black and white logo should be used when printing in black and white or in contexts where the primary logo may not be visible.
Tertiary May be used on dark backgrounds where the secondary logo may not be visible.
Colorful May be used if a more colorful logo is desired. For example, homepages and full color brochures.
Colorful with Dropshadow May be used if a more emphasis is desired on the colorful logo.
Favicon The favicon should only be used only as favicons and should not appear elsewhere on the site or in print materials.
