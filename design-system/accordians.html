Accordian

Can be used to show grouped content on portfolio pages, such as Key Takeaways, Findings, Results, Summaries, etc.

  • Max-width: 690px (note: accordian will span the full width of the content area of the layout, which is a max width of 690px)
  • Margin-top: mt-5
  • Margin-bottom: mb-5
